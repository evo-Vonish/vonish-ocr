# Brand Spirit

**Lantern in Hand (掌灯取证)**

The brand kernel of VonishOCR can be expressed in four characters. Not a slogan. A stance.

---

## Core Sentence

> The light is in your hand. It does not belong to the cloud. It does not judge for you.

This means two things:

1. **Sovereignty**: your files, your models, your results. All in your hand. No third party sustains your workflow.
2. **Illumination shared**: VonishOCR is open source. You use it, improve it, distribute it. The lantern lights others.

---

## Brand Temperament

| Keyword | Meaning | Visual Expression |
|---------|---------|-------------------|
| Industrial | Precise, unadorned, function-first | Sharp corners, micro-radii, no gradients, no shadows |
| Archival | Serious, retrievable, auditable | Mono readouts, structured info, numbering systems |
| Precise | Accurate, controllable, measurable | Values to two decimals, status indicators, bounding boxes |
| Sovereign | User in control, transparent, no concealment | Diff review, low-confidence marking, preprocessing logs |

---

## Brand Rejections

- SaaS blue, gradients, high-saturation colors (brand color is one: Cyan-Lamp #8FF6D2)
- Border radii larger than 8px
- Cartoon, hand-drawn, anthropomorphic elements
- Corporate language ("we", "our company", "smart")
- Emoji — use SVG or mono symbols instead

---

> Brand is not skin. It is bone. Every pixel, every sentence, every interaction decision must pass the four-character test: 掌灯取证.
