# VonishOCR

**Local-first OCR. Light held in your own hand.**

VonishOCR is not a cloud service that swallows your files and returns clean text. It is a tool you hold. A lantern you point at the blurred, the faded, the crumpled — and watch as letters emerge from the ruin.

---

## What It Is

VonishOCR runs entirely on your machine. The OCR engine, the AI refiner, the preprocessing pipeline — all local. No file uploads. No subscription tiers. No "we'll improve your result on our servers."

You hand it a photograph of a creased receipt, a scan of a water-damaged book page, a screenshot with compression artifacts. It classifies the scene, picks the right tools, runs the pipeline, and hands you the text — marked where it is uncertain, highlighted where you should look again.

---

## Three Principles

| Principle | Meaning |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sovereignty** | Files stay on device. Models run locally. You control the evidence chain. |
| **Visible Work** | Every step — upload, preprocess, recognize, audit — is rendered as a visible transition. No magic. |
| **Honest Error** | Low-confidence fields, skew, fracture, blur are marked, not smoothed away. |

---

## Who It Is For

- Archivists digitizing fragile documents
- Researchers working with scanned historical texts
- Developers who need OCR in their local toolchain
- Anyone who refuses to hand their files to a third party
